# 2. Project Overview
The Micro_Invasion AR is an augmented reality experience created for Snapchat that brings microscopic worlds to life. Users can interact with 3D models and explore a virtual microscopic invasion through their phone camera.

---

# 3. My Role
- Designed and developed the AR lens using Snapchat Lens Studio
- Created 3D models for AR interaction
- Implemented JavaScript-based interactions
- Optimized for mobile AR performance

---

# 4. Core Contributions

## 4.1 AR Lens Development
The project features a fully interactive AR lens that tracks facial features and hand movements to create immersive experiences.

![AR Lens](/projects/micro-invasion/images/cover.png)

This system solves the challenge of making AR accessible through social media platforms.

---

## 4.2 3D Modeling and Interaction
The AR experience includes detailed 3D models that respond to user interactions in real-time.

- **Face Tracking**: Advanced facial recognition for AR placement
- **Interactive Elements**: Touch and gesture-based interactions
